Most entry-level web developers, UX/UI specialists and designers consider themselves advocates, having focused studies on web accessibility and ADA compliance, while leaving the business goals on the backburner for someone else to worry about. But businesses need money to survive.There's no point in having a sleek website with joyful users if they are not converting into sales. A good designer and developer must make sure that the business goals and the user goals live in harmony and cooperation.
