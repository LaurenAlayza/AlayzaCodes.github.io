Background

Lifelong learner, coder, & civic advocate.

Hi! Thanks for checking out my portfolio. My name is Lauren Alayza and I am a web and software developer. I first began my coding journey in May 2020. Before I learned to code, I dabbled in other industries which have helped me become well-rounded in hard and soft skills. I am a classically trained cellist and performer; music has and always will be an important aspect of my life. In addition, I serve as Secretary of the Indiana Latino Democratic Caucus. I first began volunteering with the Democrats in 2008 when Obama was running for office, before I could even vote.

I studied Political Science at Indiana University, and in my senior year of my undergraduate degree I discovered an interest in technology and its effects on society. I became curious about how technology could be used for good, and how to sustainably produce and power it as well. I studied emerging green technologies, and about the intersection of technology and government. There's an adage that goes like, "Be the change you wish to see in the world"
